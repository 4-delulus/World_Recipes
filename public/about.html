Our Story

Four friends in university shared a common problem - deciding what to eat. They often found themselves spending more time arguing over where to eat than actually enjoying their meal. Frustrated by their indecisiveness and pickiness, they decided to do something about it.

With their combined skills in programming and passion for food, they set out to create an app that would revolutionize the way people approach meal decisions. Their goal was simple: to help indecisive or picky individuals discover new foods and connect through cuisines.

After months of hard work and dedication, the app was born. It provided users with personalized recommendations based on their preferences, dietary restrictions, and location. Whether craving a specific cuisine or open to trying something new, the app had something for everyone.

Today, their app has helped thousands of people overcome their food dilemmas, bringing joy and excitement back into mealtime. But their journey doesn't end here - they continue to innovate and inspire others to explore the rich and diverse world of food.
